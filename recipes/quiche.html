Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 4 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9-inch pie pan.
  2. Raw ingredients for quiche including bacon, cheese, and eggs.
  3. Allrecipes / Sonia Bozzo
  4. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  5. Strips of bacon cooking in a frying pan on stovetop.
  6. Allrecipes / Sonia Bozzo
  7. Drain bacon slices on paper towels. Crumble and set aside.
  8. Cooked bacon draining on paper towel next to crumbles.
  9. Cover bottom of prepared pie pan with shredded cheese and crumbled bacon.
  10. Shredded cheese and bacon layered in pie dish.
  11. Whisk together milk, eggs, flour, onion, butter, and salt in a large bowl until smooth. Pour mixture over cheese and bacon in pie pan.
  12. Unbaked quiche mixture with visible bacon and onions.
  13. Bake quiche in the center of the preheated oven until golden brown and firm in the center, about 35 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
